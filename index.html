Our Locations

Cremation & Burial Grounds

Four dedicated facilities serving Latur's Hindu and Muslim communities with dignity and care.

🕊️

Kabristaan - Peer Haji

Muslim burial ground near Peer Haji Dargah. Well-maintained with separate sections for men, women and children.

📍 Peer Haji Road, Latur · 1.2 km from city center
🕊️

Kabristaan - Barshi Road

Largest Muslim burial ground in Latur. Located on Barshi Road with ample parking and prayer facilities.

📍 Barshi Road, Latur · 3.5 km from city center
🏥

Shamshan Ghat - Main

Primary Hindu cremation ground with modern electric crematorium and traditional pyre facilities.

📍 Old City, Latur · 1.5 km from city center
🏥

Shamshan Ghat - Ausa Road

Secondary Hindu cremation ground on Ausa Road. Features waiting hall, water supply and seating arrangements.

📍 Ausa Road, Latur · 4.0 km from city center
